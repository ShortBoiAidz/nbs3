Irenastos

Name:

Stemming from the Greek words "Eirene" (peace) and "Nanos" (dwarf), Irenastos was built for the vertically challenged, with most builds being 1 block tall.

History:

Irenastos was originally a hemp farm built by ShortBoiAidz 4000 blocks from spawn to hide from Emorythefem. However, after a few weeks of farming, ShortBoiAidz decided to expand the farm into a society for origins of 1 block tall height. The society was very small to begin with, but on April 11th, 2026, ShortBoiAidz expanded the territory from a few thousand blocks to over 4 million blocks. It is now known as the largest country in NoodleBox, and is home to 5 citizens, including the founder, ShortBoiAidz.

Politics:

Irenastos is an anarchist society based on the political ideology of Reciprocal Communalism, which emphasizes mutual aid and cooperation among its citizens. The society operates on a system of communal ownership and decision-making, where resources and responsibilities are shared among all members. Decisions are made through a direct democracy of all citizens. No one person has more power than another, and everyone is expected to contribute to the society in some way. Ones who do not contribute to society do not receive the benefits of society, such as access to communal resources.
